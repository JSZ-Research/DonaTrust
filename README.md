## üåü Our Mission
In remote areas, students with visual and hearing impairments often face a double barrier: geographical isolation and communication silence. **DonaTrust** is a decentralized donation platform designed to rewrite this narrative. 

By leveraging blockchain technology, we ensure every drop of kindness is transparent and traceable. More importantly, we use technology to translate gratitude‚Äîconverting hand signs and diverse languages into a visual bridge that connects the hearts of donors and beneficiaries.

### Why "CTRL+HACK+DEL 2.0"?
- **CTRL (Control):** Empowering disabled students to take control of their own destinies.
- **HACK (Hack):** Using technology to hack through the lack of transparency in traditional charity.
- **DEL (Delete):** Deleting the barriers of distance, sensory impairment, and information asymmetry.

---

## ‚ú® Key Features
- **Decentralized Trust:** Built on blockchain technology to ensure that 100% of donations reach the intended students without intermediaries.
- **Visual Gratitude:** An integrated translation engine for sign language and multiple dialects, allowing beneficiaries to express their thanks in their own "voice."
- **Artistic Connection:** A high-aesthetic interface designed to treat charity not just as a transaction, but as a soulful human connection.
- **Remote Education Support:** Focused specifically on educational resources for the blind, deaf, and mute in underserved regions.
